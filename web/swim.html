SWIMMING





The club has a swimming pool of international size and standards.SR Club houses a huge swimming
pool where coaching facilities are provided throughout the year. Not just that, we have smaller
swimming poolfor children along with spring and diving boards respectively. There is an annual club
aquatic meet with innovative water events and expert coaches to train swimmers in various styles and water
sports. With Olympic size pool and most modern infrastructure and equipments, this complex also provides
swimming training by trainers and it also allows other organizations to manage and events and competitions.
The prices vary accordingly tothe age group, for age group of less than 16 years 1500 rupee per month is the fees whereas
for more than 16 years of age 2500 rupees is the monthly fees.
