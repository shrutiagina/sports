About Us...!




At SR Sports Club, an effort has been made to provide world class facilities and infrastructure for the promotion of sports and health. During the past few years SR Sports Club has emerged as one of the best facilities in Kerala in the area of sports and health and is making strides to acquire a formidable presence in the international arena also.
SR Club located in the Kakkanad , Kochi is leaping towards boundless glory and fame.The club with most modern infrastructure, trainers , grounds , Olympic level swimming pool and a badminton court set up has excellent landscape abundant with all the sports equipments.
The SR Club is a platform that provides a large ground to play sports like cricket,football and volleyball ,Also A Badminton court , BasketBall Court And a swimming pool , where people can practice and organizations can conduct competitions.
