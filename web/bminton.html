BADMINTON





The Badminton court at SR club is not less than a professionally prepared court, it is well equipped
and well lit for evening practice sessions too. So if you are bitten by a badminton bug, you know where to come!
The SR Badminton Court, based in Kakkannad Kochi. It is a training and recreation centre for all
who share the passion for Badminton - play your match, unwind, relax and enjoy as the club accommodates all
standards of player from beginners to serious team players.
In this endeavor SR club conducts coaching camps, other badminton related activities and holds
local tournaments to identify and recognize talented players.

Facilities

• Wooden Court for safe and injury free sport

• Flood Lighting for great indoor performance

• Infrastructure optimized to eliminate heat

• Spectator gallery to sit back and enjoy the game
